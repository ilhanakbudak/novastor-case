# Billing and Pricing

## Billing model
Storage is billed per cubic meter (m3) per month, based on the contracted quota. Pick-and-pack
and last-mile services are billed per order line and per delivery respectively.

## Contract tiers
- Standard: base storage rate, business-hours support.
- Premium: discounted storage rate above 1,000 m3, priority support, included climate
  monitoring.
- Enterprise: custom pricing, dedicated account team, fastest support and provisioning.

## Quota overage
If monthly storage usage exceeds the contracted quota, the overage is billed at 1.5 times the
standard per-m3 rate for the portion above quota. NovaStor notifies customers when usage
reaches 90 percent of quota.

## Invoicing
Invoices are issued monthly, in arrears, with payment due net 30 days. Outstanding invoices are
visible in the portal. Persistent late payment may lead to suspension of additional
provisioning.

## Insurance surcharge
Coverage above the default 100,000 EUR is billed as a monthly surcharge proportional to the
declared value.
