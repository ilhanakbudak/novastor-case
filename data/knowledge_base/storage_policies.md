# Storage Policies

## Access hours
Warehouse access for customer staff is available on business days from 07:00 to 19:00.
Out-of-hours access can be arranged with 48 hours notice.

## Security
All distribution centers have 24/7 monitored CCTV, access-controlled entry, and on-site
security personnel. Access to bonded areas is additionally logged for customs compliance.

## Hazardous goods
Storage of hazardous or dangerous goods (ADR classes) requires prior written approval and is
only available at certified sites. Undeclared hazardous goods will be refused.

## Insurance
Goods are insured against fire, theft, and water damage up to a default of 100,000 EUR per
customer. Higher coverage is available on request and is reflected in the contract. Customers
are responsible for declaring high-value goods.

## Requesting additional space
Additional storage space is requested through the customer portal. Standard provisioning lead
time is 5 business days. Provisioning may be faster for Enterprise-tier customers.

## Minimum contract term
The minimum storage contract term is 12 months. Early termination is subject to the terms of
the Master Service Agreement.
