# NovaStor Service Catalog

NovaStor Logistics offers the following warehousing and logistics services to corporate
customers.

## Standard Storage
Ambient-temperature pallet and shelf storage in secured distribution centers. Suitable for
non-perishable goods. Billed per cubic meter per month.

## Climate-Controlled Storage
Temperature- and humidity-regulated storage for sensitive goods such as electronics,
pharmaceuticals, and food. Maintained at 15-22 degrees Celsius and 45-55 percent relative
humidity. Continuous monitoring with alerting on excursions.

## Bonded / Customs Storage
Customs-bonded warehousing for imported goods on which duty has not yet been paid. Allows
deferral of import duties until goods leave the bonded area.

## Inventory Management
Real-time stock tracking, cycle counting, and low-stock alerts integrated with the customer
portal.

## Pick-and-Pack Fulfilment
Order picking, packing, and labeling for direct-to-customer or B2B distribution. Billed per
order line.

## Last-Mile Delivery
Regional delivery from NovaStor distribution centers to final destinations, with tracking and
proof of delivery.

## Cross-Docking
Inbound shipments are sorted and shipped out with minimal storage time, reducing handling and
holding costs for high-turnover goods.
