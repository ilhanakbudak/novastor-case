# Service Level Agreement and Support

## Support tiers
Support response targets depend on the customer's contract tier.

- Standard tier: critical incidents acknowledged within 4 business hours; standard requests
  within 1 business day.
- Premium tier: critical incidents acknowledged within 1 hour, 24/7; standard requests within
  4 business hours.
- Enterprise tier: critical incidents acknowledged within 30 minutes, 24/7, with a dedicated
  response team; standard requests within 2 business hours.

## Climate excursion handling
For climate-controlled storage, any temperature or humidity excursion outside the agreed range
triggers an automatic alert. On Premium and Enterprise contracts, NovaStor staff begin
remediation immediately and notify the customer within 1 hour. Excursion reports are available
in the portal.

## Portal availability
The customer portal targets 99.5 percent monthly uptime. Planned maintenance is announced at
least 48 hours in advance.

## Incident reporting
Incidents can be raised through the portal or by contacting the customer's account manager.
Each incident receives a ticket ID for tracking.
