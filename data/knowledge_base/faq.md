# Frequently Asked Questions

**How is my storage billed?**
By cubic meter per month against your contracted quota. Usage above quota is billed at 1.5x the
standard rate for the excess.

**What happens if I exceed my quota?**
You are billed the overage rate for the excess volume. We notify you when usage reaches 90
percent of quota so you can request more space.

**How do I add storage space?**
Submit a request in the customer portal. Standard provisioning takes 5 business days.

**Is my inventory insured?**
Yes, up to 100,000 EUR by default against fire, theft, and water damage. Higher coverage is
available on request.

**How do I track a shipment?**
Shipment status and ETA are available in the portal, or you can ask the assistant for a specific
shipment by its ID.

**When does my contract renew?**
Your contract start and end dates are in your account details. The minimum term is 12 months.

**Who is my account manager?**
Your assigned account manager is shown in your account details in the portal.

**What are your warehouse access hours?**
Business days 07:00 to 19:00; out-of-hours access can be arranged with 48 hours notice.
